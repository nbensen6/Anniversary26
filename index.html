A Perfect Day Together

Sunday, January 25th, 2026 - A carefully planned winter day, just for us

Suggested Anniversary Itinerary

Late Morning
West Side Market

Start our day wandering through Cleveland's oldest public market. We'll grab fresh pastries and coffee, sample artisan cheeses, and soak in the bustling atmosphere. Head up to the balcony for a panoramic view of it all.

Early Afternoon
Cleveland Metroparks Zoo - Winter Wonder Weekends

Perfect timing for Winter Wonder Weekends! We'll enjoy free heated carousel rides, watch elephant baths, see pinniped training, and catch special keeper chats about tigers and primates. The heated Zoo Loop shuttle makes it easy to explore even in January.

Late Afternoon
SoulSpace Wellness

Unwind together at this Gordon Square wellness lounge. We'll enjoy the sauna, steam room, and cold plunge - the perfect way to relax before dinner. Their outdoor relaxation garden has cozy ambient lighting.

Evening
Dinner at 17 River Grille

Our favorite spot! Floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the stunning Chagrin Falls, white tablecloths, leather booths, and the modern American cuisine we love. There's nowhere else I'd rather celebrate with you.

Night
M Bevy

End our night just steps from dinner at this intimate cocktail bar in downtown Chagrin Falls. High-quality craft cocktails in a cozy, elevated atmosphere - the perfect nightcap spot to toast to us without the long drive back.

Winter in Cleveland

Cleveland transforms into a magical winter wonderland. The cozy indoor venues, warm restaurants, and intimate atmosphere make it perfect for a romantic anniversary celebration. Bundle up for short walks between spots - the crisp air and city lights create their own kind of magic.

Fine Dining

Cleveland's most romantic restaurants for our special night

Marble Room

A breathtaking former bank building transformed into Cleveland's most luxurious dining destination. Soaring ceilings, spectacular interiors, and expertly crafted cocktails create pure romance.

Downtown Special Occasion Cocktails
White Oaks Restaurant

Nine-time winner and six consecutive years voted #1 Most Romantic Restaurant in Cleveland. This is where Cleveland goes to celebrate love.

Award Winner Classic Romance
Pier W

A Cleveland institution since 1965, featuring a cantilevered dining room that extends over Lake Erie. Incredible views of the downtown skyline, especially stunning at sunset.

Lakewood Lake Views Seafood
Dante Restaurant

Cleveland's only restaurant with a Michelin-starred chef, Dante Boccuzzi. Located in charming Tremont, offering an elevated American menu with creative twists.

Tremont Michelin Chef
Bartleby Cleveland

An Ohio City gem in a historic 1920s building with grand vaulted ceilings, tapered candles, and whimsical touches like rabbit lamps and a charming tree inside.

Ohio City Historic Whimsical
Baricelli Inn

Tucked away in Little Italy, this intimate restaurant is also a small inn. Often called "the most romantic" spot in Cleveland for its cozy, European charm.

Little Italy Intimate Charming
17 River Grille

Floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the stunning Chagrin Falls. White tablecloths, leather booths, and modern American cuisine create an unforgettable setting.

Chagrin Falls Waterfall Views

A Cleveland institution in the Historic Warehouse District for over two decades. Sleek, polished, and perfect for oyster lovers and seafood enthusiasts.
