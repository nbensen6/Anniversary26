A Perfect Day Together

Sunday, January 26th, 2026 - A carefully planned winter day, just for us

Suggested Anniversary Itinerary

Late Morning
West Side Market

Start our day wandering through Cleveland's oldest public market. We'll grab fresh pastries and coffee, sample artisan cheeses, and soak in the bustling atmosphere. Head up to the balcony for a panoramic view of it all.

Early Afternoon
Crocker Park Ice Skating & Ice Festival

Perfect timing! January 26 is the final day of the Crocker Park Ice Festival! We'll skate together on the outdoor rink, watch live ice-sculpting demonstrations, explore 3D ice displays, and enjoy the magical winter atmosphere. Skating is $12/person including skate rental.

Late Afternoon
SoulSpace Wellness

Unwind together at this Gordon Square wellness lounge. We'll enjoy the sauna, steam room, and cold plunge - the perfect way to relax before dinner. Their outdoor relaxation garden has cozy ambient lighting.

Evening
Dinner at 17 River Grille

Our favorite spot! Floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the stunning Chagrin Falls, white tablecloths, leather booths, and the modern American cuisine we love. There's nowhere else I'd rather celebrate with you.

Night
Velvet Tango Room

End our night at this legendary speakeasy for handcrafted cocktails in an intimate, candlelit setting. It's the perfect nightcap spot to toast to us.

Winter in Cleveland

Cleveland transforms into a magical winter wonderland. The cozy indoor venues, warm restaurants, and intimate atmosphere make it perfect for a romantic anniversary celebration. Bundle up for short walks between spots - the crisp air and city lights create their own kind of magic.

Fine Dining

Cleveland's most romantic restaurants for our special night

Marble Room

A breathtaking former bank building transformed into Cleveland's most luxurious dining destination. Soaring ceilings, spectacular interiors, and expertly crafted cocktails create pure romance.

Downtown Special Occasion Cocktails
White Oaks Restaurant

Nine-time winner and six consecutive years voted #1 Most Romantic Restaurant in Cleveland. This is where Cleveland goes to celebrate love.

Award Winner Classic Romance
Pier W

A Cleveland institution since 1965, featuring a cantilevered dining room that extends over Lake Erie. Incredible views of the downtown skyline, especially stunning at sunset.

Lakewood Lake Views Seafood
Dante Restaurant

Cleveland's only restaurant with a Michelin-starred chef, Dante Boccuzzi. Located in charming Tremont, offering an elevated American menu with creative twists.

Tremont Michelin Chef
Bartleby Cleveland

An Ohio City gem in a historic 1920s building with grand vaulted ceilings, tapered candles, and whimsical touches like rabbit lamps and a charming tree inside.

Ohio City Historic Whimsical
Baricelli Inn

Tucked away in Little Italy, this intimate restaurant is also a small inn. Often called "the most romantic" spot in Cleveland for its cozy, European charm.

Little Italy Intimate Charming
17 River Grille

Floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the stunning Chagrin Falls. White tablecloths, leather booths, and modern American cuisine create an unforgettable setting.

Chagrin Falls Waterfall Views
Blue Point Grille

A Cleveland institution in the Historic Warehouse District for over two decades. Sleek, polished, and perfect for oyster lovers and seafood enthusiasts.

Warehouse District Seafood Oysters

Unique Experiences

Beyond dinner - memorable activities for us to share

Glass Bubble Project

Take a private glassblowing class together in Ohio City! We can create our own wine glasses or beer mugs. Date night workshops every Thursday and Friday at 6 PM.

Hands-On Creative Memorable
Cleveland Orchestra at Severance Hall

Experience world-class music in a grandiose art deco venue. The intricate adornments and warm lighting create the perfect ambiance for a special night out.

Music Elegant Cultural
Cleveland Museum of Art

A world-class collection spanning 6,000 years - and it's completely free! The glass atrium is a perfect spot for quiet conversation. Let's find our favorite pieces together.

Art Free Romantic
SoulSpace Wellness

A Gordon Square wellness lounge where we can unwind together. Sauna, steam room, and cold plunge for about $50 for 2 hours. Their outdoor garden has ambient lighting and cozy loungers.

Relaxation Wellness Couples
Rock and Roll Hall of Fame

Explore the history of rock and roll together. Even if we've been before, there's always something new to discover. Perfect for music lovers!

Music History Iconic
Aut-O-Rama Drive-In

Old-fashioned charm at a drive-in that's been around since the 60s. Movies under the stars from the comfort of our car - nostalgia at its finest.

Nostalgic Movies Cozy

Hidden Gems

Secret spots and unexpected delights waiting for us

Velvet Tango Room

A legendary speakeasy with intimate, candlelit vibes. Handcrafted cocktails in one of Cleveland's best-kept secrets. Perfect for ending our night.

Speakeasy Cocktails Intimate
Mahall's 20 Lanes

Retro bowling in Lakewood with live music, dancing, and great food. Their basement lounge "Pins and Needles" has 70s-inspired decor - a true hidden gem.

Lakewood Bowling Retro
LBM Viking Bar

A Viking-themed bar in Lakewood with bold drinks like "Ocean of Wounds" and "Blood Eagle." Dark vibes and a killer playlist for something completely different.

Unique Themed Adventure
Buckland Museum of Witchcraft

An off-the-beaten-path adventure featuring crystal balls, enchanted cloaks, and stories of witches. For when we want something truly unexpected!

Quirky Unique Adventure
Super Electric Pinball Parlour

Vintage pinball machines for a playful, competitive date night. A retro atmosphere that's perfect for some friendly competition between us.

Games Retro Fun
Le Petit Triangle

A hidden gem serving authentic French cuisine at affordable prices. Perfect for a cozy brunch or intimate dinner in Ohio City.

French Hidden Gem Ohio City
Cloak & Dagger

A dark, cozy Tremont bar with the aesthetic of an author's study. Designer cocktails and a distinctive all-vegan menu for something unique.

Tremont Cozy Vegan
Lakewood Solstice Steps

Masonry stairs along Lake Erie, oriented to point directly at the setting sun on the summer solstice. Even in winter, the sunset views are magical.

Scenic Sunset Free
